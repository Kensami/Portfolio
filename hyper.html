City Train

Medium: After Effects

For my Hyper Reality project, I chose to use the video “City train driving under a bridge” by Edgar Fernandez for my inspiration. My goal was to capture a sense of expanding your horizons and living life to the fullest through typography and motion. I layered text and a video footage of a man breakdancing on the train. Luckily, I was able to encompass the action of movement and create the beautiful process of living ordinary to actively engaging in new experiences.
